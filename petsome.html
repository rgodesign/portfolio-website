Petsome

Petsome started with a design brief for an online storefront. I developed a style and brand guide before starting to design so that I could have a consistent brand in mind from the beginning.

Process

Research & Ideation

Petsome started with a design brief for an online storefront for users who have a hard time finding specific items in physical storefronts. I analyzed other pet supply websites and looked for ways to clean and streamline the design.

Wireframes

I made fewer iterations of Petsome due to having less feedback throughout. Instead, feedback came later in the process this time. A prototype was made using the high-fidelity wireframes and tested on users, who remarked on how easy it was to use.

Design

Style and brand were central to Petsome’s design. Its bright, friendly visual style was at the center of my vision from the beginning, and the brand’s focus was always on putting animals in the spotlight.

Conclusion

With Petsome I was quickly able to develop a brand and a vision. As I designed it, I strived to make it increasingly clean and minimal. The results of my user tests confirm that this goal was accomplished.
