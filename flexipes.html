Flexipes

Flexipes is a responsive web app where users can find recipes with simple, flexible ingredients. Users can search for recipes using filters for ingredients, preparation methods, and dietary restrictions.

Process

Research & Ideation

Flexipes was based on extensive user research and competitor analysis. Starting from the base idea of making a recipe app, I interviwed multiple potential users and developed user personas and flows.

Wireframes

I first designed Flexipes through rapid sketching on paper before going digital in Sketch. It started with a home screen at a mobile breakpoint, then expanded to other screens and breakpoints.

Design

Flexipes has a casual design to reflect its more approachable style of cooking. User preference testing was done to refine the style of the design, in addition to other feedback. The final mockups consisted of 5 screens across 4 different breakpoints.

Conclusion

Flexipes continued to go through iterations and changes based on feedback from users and designers. It was a valuable learning experience, especially in regards to research, planning, and implementing feedback.
