About Me

I’m a UI designer with a background in psychological and social services. My time working in food service also means I have experience at working with a team to stick to high standards and deliver results, even under pressure. I have an eye for detail that is based in being empathetic about helping people get exactly what they want—which translates directly into user-focused design.

I have a lifelong love of visual arts and design that I have been cultivating into a career. I have certifications in Multimedia Design from Sessions College for Professional Design and immersive UI design from CareerFoundry. I’m ready to bring my unique combination of skills and experience to a team where I can create designs that will make a difference in users’ lives.
